Ultima Lucha
PromotionsLucha Underground
First eventUltima Lucha 1

Ultima Lucha (Spanish for "Final Fight") was a professional wrestling event that aired as the season finale of the Lucha Underground television series. The event was created in 2015 to be Lucha Underground's premier event of the year, similar to WWE's WrestleMania event.

History[edit]

On March 25, 2015, it was announced that Lucha Underground would be holding their first major event in early August 2015 entitled Ultima Lucha.[1]
