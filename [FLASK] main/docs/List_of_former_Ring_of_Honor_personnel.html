From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ring of Honor (ROH) is an American professional wrestling promotion based in Baltimore, Maryland. Former employees in ROH consist of professional wrestlers, managers, play-by-play and color commentators, announcers, interviewers, and referees.[1]
