This WikiProject was formed to foster better articles on the U.S. State of West Virginia, with a spirit of cooperation. This page and its sub-pages aim to both provide resources for Wikipedia, and expand Wikipedia as a resource about West Virginia. The project's talk page is a place for members to discuss issues regarding project-related articles. To join, simply add your name to the list at the participants section. To see articles and information about West Virginia, see the West Virginia Portal.

Project divisions[edit]

  • Communities (talk) – Cities, towns, and villages
  • Physical geography (talk) – Regions, parks, climate, physical features
  • Transportation (talk) – Roads, trails, rail, ferries, and airplanes in and around West Virginia
  • Culture (talk) – Music, art, icons, mass communication
  • People (talk) – Includes West Virginia natives and people from the current 55 counties pre-statehood
  • Government (talk) – Government: federal, state and local politics in relation to West Virginia
  • History (talk) – History of West Virginia during and prior to statehood
  • Economy (Talk) – Major West Virginia industries, employers, trends, and history relating to the economy of Virginia
  • Education (Talk) – All schools, colleges, universities and educational organizations & institutions in West Virginia
