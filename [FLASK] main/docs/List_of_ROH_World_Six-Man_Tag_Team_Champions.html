Listing of professional wrestling champions for the ROH World Six-Man Tag Team Championship

The ROH World Six-Man Tag Team Championship is a professional wrestling championship owned by the Ring of Honor (ROH) promotion. Like most professional wrestling championships, the title is won as a result of a match with a predetermined outcome.

Current champions Bullet Club Gold
(Jay White, Austin Gunn, and Colten Gunn)

Bullet Club Gold (Jay White and The Gunns (Austin Gunn and Colten Gunn)) are the current champions in their first reign as a team and individually, They defeated Mogul Embassy (Brian Cage, Bishop Kaun, and Toa Liona) on January 17, 2024 in North Charleston, South Carolina at Dynamite to win the titles.

There have been 17 reigns shared among 12 teams and 32 wrestlers. The Kingdom (Matt Taven, T. K. O'Ryan and Vinny Marseglia) were the inaugural champions and they have the record with the most reigns at three as a team, while individually Vinny Marseglia/Vincent has the most reigns with four.
