"Ahora Me Llama" is a song by Colombian singer Karol G and Puerto Rican rapper Bad Bunny, released on May 26, 2017.[1]

Music video[edit]

The video of "Ahora Me Llama" was released on June 8, 2017 on Karol G's YouTube channel.[2] As of December 2023, the music video for the song has over 1 billion views on YouTube.
