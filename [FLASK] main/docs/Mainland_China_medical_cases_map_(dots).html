No active cases Confirmed Imported[a] First outbreak of pandemic (no active cases)

As of 5 July 2021[update], there have been 2951 total (200 active) imported cases in mainland China, with no deaths recorded among them:[5]

  • Anhui: 1 total imported case (all recovered).[6]
  • Beijing: 176 total imported cases (1 active).[7]
  • Chongqing: 9 total imported cases (1 active).[8]
  • Fujian: 118 total imported cases (14 active[b]).[9]
  • Gansu: 78 total imported cases (all recovered).[10]
  • Guangdong: 452 total imported cases (25 active[c]).[11]
  • Guangxi: 8 total imported cases (3 active[d]).[12]
  • Guizhou: 1 total imported case (all recovered).[13]
  • Hebei: 26 total imported cases (1 active[e]).[14]
  • Heilongjiang: 387 total imported cases (all recovered).[15]
  • Henan: 8 total imported cases (4 active[f]).[16]
  • Hunan: 1 total imported case (all recovered).[17]
  • Inner Mongolia: 191 total imported cases (6 active[g]).[18]
  • Jiangsu: 22 total imported cases (all recovered).[19]
  • Jiangxi: 2 total imported cases (all recovered).[h].[20]
  • Jilin: 19 total imported cases (3 active)[i].[21]
  • Liaoning: 21 total imported cases (all recovered).[22]
  • Shaanxi: 63 total imported cases (8 active).[23]
  • Shandong: 25 total imported cases (2 active).[24]
  • Shanghai: 321 total imported cases (22 active).[25]
  • Shanxi: 64 total imported cases (all recovered).[26]
  • Sichuan: 21 total imported cases (all recovered)[j].[27]
  • Tianjin: 55 total imported cases (2 active).[28]
  • Yunnan: 11 total imported cases (all recovered).[29]
  • Zhejiang: 50 total imported cases (all recovered).[30]


See "Notes" section below for imported cases details (all data are given out of total imported cases).
