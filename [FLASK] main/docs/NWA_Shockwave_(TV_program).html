NWA Shockwave is a professional wrestling streaming television program produced by the National Wrestling Alliance (NWA). The show began airing on December 1, 2020 on NWA's YouTube channel.[1] The series primarily featured matches taped during United Wrestling Network's weekly pay-per-view series, UWN Primetime Live.
