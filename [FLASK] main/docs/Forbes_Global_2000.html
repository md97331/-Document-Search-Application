From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Annual ranking of the top 2000 public companies published by Forbes magazine

The Forbes Global 2000 is an annual ranking of the top 2000 public companies in the world, published by Forbes magazine. "The Global 2000" annual ranking is assembled by Forbes using a weighted assessment of four metrics: sales, profit, assets and market value.[1] The list has been published annually since 2003.
