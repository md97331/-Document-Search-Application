This is a list of cathedrals in the Philippines sorted by denomination.

Roman Catholic[edit]

See also: Category:Roman Catholic cathedrals in the Philippines

The Catholic Church in the Philippines is organized into 72 dioceses in 16 ecclesiastical provinces, as well as seven apostolic vicariates and a military ordinariate. These are the following Roman Catholic cathedrals in the Philippines:[1]
