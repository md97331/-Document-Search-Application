NWA Special Supercards is a group of professional wrestling supercard events scripted and promoted by the National Wrestling Alliance (NWA) in conjunction with various independent professional wrestling promotions NWA President William Patrick Corgan explained the concept as "being able to work, show up anywhere in the world that appreciates moments", and partnering with local promotions.

The difference between PPVs and Supercards its that PPV Events are aired live on FITE TV - Triller TV and Supercards are tapings for future Power episodes or Youtube TV Specials.

  • NWA Pop-Up Events
    • The inaugural event, New Years Clash, was held on January 5, 2019, and co-produced with Crimson's Tried-N-True Pro Wrestling in Clarksville, Tennessee, and was aired live on a PPV before the introduction of NWA Power.[1]
  • NWA PowerrrTrip Events
    • Since 2022 the event series change to NWA PowerrrTrip with taping matches for a future episodes of NWA Power.[2]
  • NWA Signature Live Events
    • Since 2024 NWA starts partership with CW Network and the past PPV events now will be called Signature Live Events with matches taped and aired for the future NWA Power episodes.
